Sonic Robo Blast 2: The Disaster

Sonic Robo Blast 2: The Disaster is a BLua modification of the incredibly famous Sonic the Hedgehog fan game Sonic Robo Blast 2. The goal is to recreate the experience of Sonic.EXE ASYMS such as Sonic.EXE: The Disaster: 2D, Sonic.EXE: The Disaster, and Sonic.EXE: Outcome Memories, as well as other sources of inspiration. While also adding our own twist and mantaining what keeps SRB2 fun.
